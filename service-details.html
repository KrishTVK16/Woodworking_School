Course Overview

This course is designed to take the fear out of sharp tools. You will learn how to sharpen chisels and planes to a razor edge, how to layout joinery with precision, and how to execute the three foundational joints: the Dovetail, the Mortise & Tenon, and the Bridle Joint.

What You'll Build

1. Joiner's Mallet

Your first tool. Built from tough Beech and Walnut, customized to your grip.

2. Dovetail Box

A keepsake box featuring hand-cut through dovetails. The ultimate test of patience.

Tools & Skills

  • Hand Plane Setup
  • Layout Tools & Marking
  • Sawing Mechanics
  • Glue-up Strategies
