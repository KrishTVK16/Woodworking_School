Plan Your Trip

Workshop Visit Guide

Before you head over, here's everything you need to know for a smooth and safe visit to the Woodcraft Academy.

Safety First

Closed-toe shoes are mandatory for workshop entry. We provide safety glasses and hearing protection for all visitors. Please tie back long hair.

Parking & Access

Free street parking is available on Artisan Avenue. For longer stays, use the secure lot on 12th Street, just a 2-minute walk from the main entrance.

Photos & Media

Photos are encouraged in the gallery and showroom! For the privacy of our students, we ask that you avoid filming during active class hours.
