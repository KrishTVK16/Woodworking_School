The Timber Library

Understanding the building blocks of fine furniture.

Red Oak
Open-grained, strong, and cooperative. The staple of traditional American joinery.
Black Walnut
Rich, dark, and sophisticated. Known for its exceptional stability and stunning luster.
Rock Maple
Dense, hard-wearing, and brilliant white. Perfect for bench tops and high-traffic tools.
Cherry
Warm and aging beautifully. It darkens over time with exposure to light, like a fine wine.
